Research Projects

Comparing the long-term profitability of the Streaming Industry and the Movie Theatre Industry

The project aims to demonstrate that investing in the movie streaming
industry is a smarter choice than investing in the movie theater industry,
especially in light of the Covid pandemic. The target audience for this
presentation is prospective stock market investors in the U.S. and people
who have never invested in the stock market before. The presentation focuses
on Netflix, the most popular streaming service in the country, and the most
popular movie theaters, AMC and Cinemark. To determine which factors make a
company's stock more valuable, the project examines yearly revenue, yearly
profit, and trends in share value over the past four years using data from
Yahoo Finance. The impact of the pandemic on these figures is also shown through a line graph.

Determining which socioeconomic factors have the most correlation with COVID-19 contraction

The research aims to determine the socioeconomic factors that have
the most significant relationship with COVID-19 contraction, particularly
in terms of race, gender, ethnicity, and financial status. The dataset used
was gathered from the US CDC, representing the number of COVID-19 cases in
the US according to various factors. The research also considers the findings
of Peters (2020) and Patel et al (2020) that different measures should be
adopted for different regions and that more factors must be considered when
calculating the level of vulnerability of people to the virus.

Overview of Netflix's development

This research looked at Netflix's development during the periods of
2017-2021. The study explores the history of Netflix, its transition to
streaming, and its growth into a global media company. The research also
examines the key factors that have contributed to Netflix's success,
including its innovative business model, its focus on original content,
and its use of data analytics to inform content decisions. The study provides
valuable insights into the strategies that have helped Netflix become one of
the most successful companies in the entertainment industry.
